Johns Hopkins Library Search

Search Catalyst for books, articles, journals, digital media, and more within the Library's collections plus subscription databases and journal collections.

Catalyst also helps you manage your research. Sign in to:

  • Request items from JH Libraries, or via BorrowDirect or Interlibrary Loan for pickup
  • Renew checked out materials
  • Create lists of bookmarked items
  • Export citations to popular citation managers
  • View full search results (some databases only show results when you're signed in)

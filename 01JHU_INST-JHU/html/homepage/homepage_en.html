What am I searching?

Catalyst is where you search for and request books, articles, digital media, and more within JH Libraries collections, subscription databases, and journal collections.

Sign in to:

  • Request items from JH Libraries, or via BorrowDirect or Interlibrary Loan.
  • Renew checked out materials
  • Create lists of bookmarked items
  • Export citations to popular citation managers
  • View full search results (some databases only show results when you're signed in)
